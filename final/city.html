About Ogden

Ogden was the first settlement in Utah, and today is a notoriously independent community with hundreds of locally owned restaurants, bars and retailers. Located 35 miles north of Salt Lake City, Ogden is the gateway to Snowbasin and Powder Mountain ski resorts. This city is a hot spot for skiing, high adventure sports, shopping, a bustling nightlife as well as delectable dining.
