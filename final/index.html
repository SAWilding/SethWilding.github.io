Our Purpose

Founded in 1887, the Ogden-Weber Chamber of Commerce is a nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing business and community prosperity in northern Utah. Based in Ogden, the Chamber represents 775+ organizations in 15 cities and several unincorporated areas in Weber County.

Our Goals

  • Drive commerce
  • Foster unity
  • Empower members
  • Advocate for businesses
  • Enhance community engagement
  • Espouse integrity
